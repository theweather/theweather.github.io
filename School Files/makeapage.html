Creating a basic webpage on Github:

1) Create account on Github. Add your name and photograph.
2) Using VSCode or other tool, make an HTML page called index.html display your photo and your name, with content in the following format, with "Wanda" replaced with your own name.:
<html>
    <h1> Wanda's Portfolio</h1>
    <img src="./wanda.jpg"/>
</html>

3) Create a repository on Github titled with your username like so: username.github.io (eg scarletwitch.github.io)
4) Upload index.html and your photo to your repository.
5) Go to https://username.github.io (like my page ) and see if the page displays the way you expect.
